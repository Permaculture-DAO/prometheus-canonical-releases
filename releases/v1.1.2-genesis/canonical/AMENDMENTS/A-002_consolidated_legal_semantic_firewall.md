# Canon Amendment A-002 — Consolidated Legal Semantic Firewall table

- **Status:** PROPOSED (AI-drafted; pending guardian/steward ratification — the canon
  is substantially single-source and AI holds no authority).
- **Type:** ordinary consolidating amendment. Does **not** introduce new meaning; it
  gathers definitions already in the WP v1.1.2 (X.1 token table, §13.16.2 semantic
  firewall, and body) into one hostile-reviewer-friendly table.
- **Date:** 2026-06-20. **Applies to:** canonical White Paper v1.1.2.
- **Problem solved:** Phase 4 found the 15 master-prompt firewall terms present but
  **distributed**. No single `term → canonical definition → forbidden interpretation`
  table exists. This consolidates them. Prefer-amend test: consolidation, not
  addition of new normative content.

## Consolidated Legal Semantic Firewall (term → canonical definition → forbidden interpretation)
| Term | Canonical definition | Forbidden interpretation |
|---|---|---|
| **PRU** | Prometheus Regenerative Unit: an analytical index of verified regenerative performance under approved methodology (like a rating). | A right to land, cash flow, governance, securities, token, or market price; proof of value before admissibility. |
| **RAP** | Regenerative Asset Pool: portfolio aggregation logic over eligible PRU-relevant units. | A security, fund interest, or pooled investment; a source of liquidity. |
| **TRBK** | A consumptive utility/access credit, redeemable for defined services, functional at point of sale. | A security, investment, appreciation claim, yield, dividend, or revenue share. |
| **HoloFuel** | An operational network resource for transaction/coordination only. | A value or admissibility input; an investor instrument. |
| **Token** | A bounded technical/economic object whose rights depend on legal structure, governance and disclosure. | Constitutional authority; PRU/RAP admissibility; economic entitlement by itself. |
| **Governance right** | Reviewable human/institutional authorization, operational in scope. | Power to set canonical meaning, PRU/RAP admissibility, legal boundaries, or human-dignity safeguards. |
| **Investor exposure** | A separate, disclosed, compliant security issued via a warehouse vehicle (statutory trust / reserved AIF) to qualified investors under exemptions. | Anything bundled with, or disguised as, the utility token. |
| **Land right** | A right created only by a valid, jurisdiction-specific legal instrument. | Any right implied by planting, funding, measuring, or participating in an OHE. |
| **Cash-flow right** | A contractual right to defined cash flows, created only by an enforceable instrument in the security layer. | Anything implied by PRU, RAP, token, dashboard, or participation. |
| **Ecological claim** | A statement about living-system performance carrying its evidence/admissibility status. | A validated outcome before the MRV and admissibility gates clear. |
| **Verified indicator** | A gated, provenance-anchored measurement that has cleared indicator validation. | Proof of value; site validation transferred from external literature. |
| **Admissible value** | Value recognised only after every binding gate (V_base, LegalGate, MRVGate, Conf_total) returns true. | Narrative, model, token, dashboard, or AI output presented as value. |
| **Research-only material** | Frontier/Appendix-U inquiry preserved but excluded from PRU value, Conf_total, investor-facing claims and financial admissibility. Includes human-state/HSI signals (weight = 0). | An input to value, admissibility, governance, capital, or benefit eligibility. |
| **Financial instrument** | A regulated product created only through legal structuring, disclosure and competent review. | Any Prometheus analysis, token, or representation treated as such without counsel. |
| **Compliant wrapper** | A legal vehicle (e.g. Delaware statutory trust, Luxembourg RAIF, SPV/fund/note) that lawfully holds investment exposure, declared as a security. | A utility token, PRU, or RAP re-described as an investment vehicle. |
