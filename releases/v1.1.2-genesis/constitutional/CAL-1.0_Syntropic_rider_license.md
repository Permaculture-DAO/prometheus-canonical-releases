# CAL-1.0 Syntropic Rider License

## Status

This document is retained as a constitutional licensing and boundary document for the Prometheus release set. It is aligned to the v1.1 White Paper by reference and must be interpreted through the current verification-first ontology.

## Interpretive hierarchy

Where this rider interacts with Prometheus documentation, the following hierarchy applies:

1. living-system integrity;
2. human dignity and non-coercion;
3. verification-first evidence discipline;
4. separation of PRU, TRBK, HoloFuel, land rights, governance rights, investor exposure and legal instruments;
5. jurisdiction-specific legal review for any financial or digital representation.

## Non-financial boundary

This rider does not create securities, investment contracts, ownership claims, revenue rights, land rights, PRU rights, token rights or investor exposure.

## Syntropic use condition

Use of Prometheus-derived materials should not invert the architecture into extractive, coercive, misleading, unverifiable or greenwashing systems.
