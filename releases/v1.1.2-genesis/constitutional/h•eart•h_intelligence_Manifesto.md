# h•eart•h intelligence Manifesto

## v1.1 alignment note

This manifesto is preserved as the ethical and philosophical layer of h•eart•h intelligence. It must be interpreted through the current Prometheus White Paper, which converts regenerative aspiration into verification-first infrastructure.

## Principle

Life flourishes through contribution. Regenerative systems therefore measure value by the increased capacity of living systems to remain fertile, resilient, coherent, productive and capable of supporting future life.

## h•eart•h intelligence

h•eart•h intelligence is intelligence rooted in the relationship between human awareness, ecological reality, technological coordination and long-term responsibility.

It does not authorize speculative financial claims. It does not override MRV. It does not replace legal review. It provides ethical orientation for the operational architecture.

## Operational translation

Ethics become infrastructure when they shape what the system is allowed to do:

- protect human dignity;
- preserve ecological truth;
- verify before valuing;
- separate governance from investor rights;
- prevent token drift;
- route capital toward living-system capacity;
- treat technology as coordination support, not sovereign authority.
